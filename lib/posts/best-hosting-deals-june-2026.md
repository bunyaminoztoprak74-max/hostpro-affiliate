## How to Maximise These Deals

1. **Lock in the longest term at sign-up** — promotional rates apply to the initial billing period. A 3-year sign-up at $2.99/month beats a 1-year sign-up at $3.99/month that renews at $29.99/month.

2. **Verify pricing at checkout** — promotional prices can change. Always confirm the final price before entering payment details.

3. **Use free trial periods** — several hosts offer 30-day money-back guarantees (Hostinger, SiteGround, Cloudways). Test the host for a month; if performance or support doesn't meet expectations, request a refund and switch.

4. **Look for hidden costs** — domain privacy, SSL upgrades, email marketing add-ons, and security tools are common upsells that can double the effective price. Check your cart before paying.
