## What You Need to Start

- A niche (your blog topic)
- A domain name (~$10/year)
- Web hosting (~$3/month)
- WordPress (free)
- About 2 hours of setup time

That's it. No coding required.

## Step 1: Choose Your Niche

Your niche determines everything — your audience, your content, and your income potential. The best niches are:

- **Specific enough** to target a clear audience
- **Profitable enough** to monetize with ads or affiliate links
- **Something you enjoy** so you can write consistently

**High-profit niches for 2026:**
- Web hosting & blogging tools
- Personal finance & investing
- Health & fitness
- AI tools & productivity
- Home improvement & DIY
- Travel on a budget

## Step 6: Monetize Your Blog

Once you have content and traffic, here's how to earn money:

### 1. Affiliate Marketing (Best for beginners)
Recommend products and earn commission. Amazon Associates pays 1-10%, while hosting affiliates pay $50–$200+ per sale.

### 2. Display Ads
Once you hit 10,000 monthly visitors, apply to Mediavine. Expect $15–$40 RPM (per 1,000 visitors).

### 3. Sponsored Posts
Brands pay $200–$2,000+ for a review or mention on your blog.

### 4. Digital Products
Sell ebooks, courses, or templates. 100% profit margin.
