## What Is Reseller Hosting?

Reseller hosting means you buy hosting capacity from a provider and resell it to your clients under your own brand. The setup typically looks like:

1. You buy a reseller account (e.g., $30/month for 50 GB disk, 500 GB bandwidth)
2. You create hosting packages for clients (e.g., Basic at $10/month, Pro at $20/month)
3. Clients access their own control panel under your brand
4. You handle billing; the reseller host handles the physical infrastructure

**Typical margin:** If you pay $30/month and manage 20 clients at $15/month each, you're earning $300/month on $30 in hosting costs — an 89% margin before your time.

## What to Look for in Reseller Hosting

### White-label Quality

True white-label means:
- **Custom nameservers** on your domain (ns1.youragency.com)
- **No mention of the parent host** anywhere clients can see
- **Branded login URL** (hosting.youragency.com)
- **Branded cPanel/client area** with your logo

Hostinger's model is less traditional but passes the white-label test — clients see your agency's branding, not Hostinger's.

### Billing Software (WHMCS)

WHMCS is the industry standard for hosting billing automation. Key features:
- Automated invoicing and payment collection
- Client portal with ticket system
- Domain registration integration
- Automated account provisioning when clients pay

A2 Hosting and InMotion include WHMCS free. Others require purchasing a separate WHMCS license ($15–$25/month).

### Resource Allocation

Look for:
- **Disk space:** 60 GB minimum to start; 100 GB+ for growth
- **Bandwidth:** 500 GB minimum; unmetered is better for predictable costs
- **RAM/CPU limits:** Important for sites with dynamic content (WooCommerce, membership sites)
- **Number of accounts:** Unlimited is better — some plans cap at 50 or 100 clients

### Step 2: Set Up White-label

1. Register nameservers at your domain registrar (ns1.yourdomain.com → parent host IP)
2. Configure WHM with your branding
3. Set up WHMCS at hosting.yourdomain.com
4. Create hosting packages matching your pricing structure

### Step 3: Migrate or Create Client Sites

Use Softaculous (if available) or manual migration for each client site. A2 Hosting and InMotion include free migration assistance.

### Step 4: Automate Billing

Connect WHMCS to Stripe or PayPal. Set up automated invoice emails, payment reminders, and account suspension for non-payment.

## Reseller Hosting FAQ

**How many clients do I need to make reseller hosting profitable?**
Even 5 clients at $20/month covers a basic reseller plan. At 15–20 clients, the revenue becomes meaningful. Most agency resellers target 30–100 clients for a worthwhile recurring income stream.

**Can I resell SSL certificates?**
Yes — most reseller hosts provide AutoSSL (Let's Encrypt) free for all client accounts. You can also resell commercial SSL certificates at a markup for clients who need EV or wildcard certificates.

**What happens if the parent host has downtime?**
Your clients' sites go down and they contact you. This is the risk of reseller hosting. Choose providers with 99.99% uptime records (SiteGround, A2 Hosting) and make sure your SLA with clients reflects realistic uptime expectations.

**Do I need a reseller license to sell hosting?**
No license is needed in most jurisdictions. You'll need to handle basic business requirements (business registration, invoicing, potentially GST/VAT depending on your location).
