## Portfolio Site Optimization Tips

Regardless of host, apply these optimizations for the fastest portfolio:

**Image optimization:**
1. Resize images to display dimensions before upload (2000px wide max for full-width)
2. Use WebP format (supported by all modern browsers)
3. Install Smush or ShortPixel plugin for automated optimization
4. Enable lazy loading for images below the fold

**Caching:**
1. Enable your host's caching (LiteSpeed Cache on Hostinger, SuperCacher on SiteGround)
2. Connect to Cloudflare free CDN if your host doesn't include one
3. Enable browser caching headers for repeat visitors

**WordPress theme selection:**
Choose lightweight portfolio themes: Astra + Elementor, Kadence, or GeneratePress. Avoid heavy themes like Divi that add unnecessary JavaScript on image-focused pages.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Should I use WordPress or Squarespace for my portfolio?**
WordPress gives more control, customization, and SEO capability. Squarespace has beautiful templates and simpler setup. For portfolios where SEO matters (finding clients organically), WordPress on quality hosting wins long-term. For portfolios primarily shared via direct links, Squarespace's simplicity may be preferable.

**How important is portfolio hosting speed for freelancer success?**
Research shows visitors form their first impression in 0.05 seconds. A slow portfolio (3+ second load time) creates a negative first impression — the opposite of what you want when clients are evaluating your professionalism. Fast hosting (under 1 second) supports the professional image you're building.

**Do I need a professional email for my portfolio?**
Yes — strongly recommended. yourname@yourdomain.com looks professional. yourname@gmail.com looks like a hobby. All our recommended portfolio hosts include professional email hosting.

**What's the best way to showcase video in a portfolio?**
Host videos on YouTube, Vimeo, or Wistia and embed them in your WordPress portfolio — don't host video files directly on your hosting server. Streaming video is bandwidth-intensive and would quickly exhaust any "unlimited" hosting bandwidth. Embedded videos load from CDN and don't impact your hosting resources.

**Can I switch from a website builder (Squarespace/Wix) to WordPress hosting?**
Yes, but it requires rebuilding your portfolio. The content can be exported but templates and layouts don't transfer. Consider the migration work before making the switch. If you're in the early stages, switching to WordPress now avoids migration later.
