## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much traffic can shared hosting handle for a blog?**
Quality shared hosting (Hostinger, SiteGround) handles 25,000-100,000 monthly blog visitors comfortably with caching enabled. The key is having effective caching — a cached WordPress blog page serves in milliseconds regardless of server tier.

**Do I need a CDN for my blog?**
Yes, especially if you have international readers. All our top blog host recommendations include free CDN. Enable it immediately — it can reduce load times by 40-60% for readers far from your server.

**Should I use WordPress or a website builder for blogging?**
WordPress is the clear choice for serious bloggers. It has the best SEO plugins (Yoast, RankMath), the most themes, the largest plugin ecosystem, and the easiest content management. Website builders are simpler but limit your long-term growth.

**What's the best theme for a WordPress blog?**
Lightweight themes load faster and look more professional: Astra (free), GeneratePress (free/$59/year), Kadence (free). Avoid heavy themes like Divi or Avada on shared hosting — they add significant load time.

**Can I use Hostinger for multiple niche blogs?**
Absolutely. Hostinger Premium supports 100 WordPress sites. Many niche bloggers run 5-20 blogs on a single Hostinger Premium account, dramatically reducing cost per site.
