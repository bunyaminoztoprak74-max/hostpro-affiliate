## How We Ranked Them

We scored five managers on security architecture and audit history (40%), price per feature (30%), and daily usability across Windows, Android, and iOS (30%). All five were used as daily drivers for at least two weeks.

## 1. NordPass — Best Overall (4.5/5)

The best balance in 2026: XChaCha20 encryption, public Cure53 audits, zero breach history, full passkey support, email masking, and breach scanning — at 1.99 USD/month, the cheapest Premium tier among the big names. The free plan allows unlimited passwords with one active device at a time.

## 2. Bitwarden — Best Free & Open Source (4.4/5)

The only fully open-source option in the top tier, with an unmatched free plan: unlimited devices at zero cost. Premium is just 10 USD/year. The interface is more utilitarian than NordPass, and email masking requires third-party services.

## 3. 1Password — Best for Teams (4.3/5)

Travel Mode (hides vaults at border crossings), excellent shared vaults, and the most polished apps. No free tier, and at 2.99 USD/month it costs 50% more than NordPass for individuals.

## 4. Proton Pass — Best Privacy Ecosystem (4.1/5)

From the Proton Mail team: Swiss jurisdiction, open source, built-in email aliases. Younger product — enterprise features and desktop apps still catching up.

## 5. LastPass — Held Back by History (3.6/5)

Feature-complete and familiar, but the 2022 vault-theft incidents linger, and it costs more than NordPass or Bitwarden. Fine if you already pay for it; hard to recommend as a new purchase. See our full [NordPass vs LastPass comparison](/blog/nordpass-vs-lastpass-2026).
