## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is a reliable hosting provider rating threshold?**
We consider 8.0/10 the minimum threshold for a "good" hosting provider recommendation. Anything below 7.0 has significant enough weaknesses that we actively advise against it. Our top picks (SiteGround, Hostinger, WP Engine, Kinsta, Cloudways) all score 9.0+.

**Are hosting providers getting better or worse over time?**
Both. SiteGround, Hostinger, and A2 Hosting have dramatically improved with infrastructure upgrades. HostGator, GoDaddy, and several mid-tier hosts have declined relative to the improving baseline as they haven't invested comparably in infrastructure.

**How do I switch hosting providers if I'm unhappy?**
Use a migration plugin (Duplicator, WP Migration, or your new host's migration tool). Most top hosts offer free migration assistance. Plan the migration for low-traffic periods. Change DNS records last to minimize downtime during the switch.

**Which provider offers the best money-back guarantee?**
DreamHost leads with 97 days — nearly 3x the industry standard of 30 days. WP Engine offers 60 days. Most others offer 30 days. The guarantee duration signals confidence in the product.

**Should I choose a local hosting provider or an international one?**
Server location matters for initial connection speed, but most major international providers (SiteGround, Hostinger, WP Engine, Kinsta) offer server locations worldwide including UK, EU, US, Asia, and Australia. Choose based on performance data, not company headquarters.
