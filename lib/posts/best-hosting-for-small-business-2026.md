## Business Email Hosting: What You Need to Know

All three recommended hosts support professional business email. The approach differs:

- **SiteGround:** Deep Google Workspace integration ($6/user/month for Google Workspace accounts)
- **Hostinger:** Email hosting included with plans (branded emails, basic webmail) or Google Workspace/Microsoft 365 integration
- **Cloudways:** No built-in email — requires Rackspace or external provider (adds $1/account/month)

For most businesses, Hostinger's included email or SiteGround's Google Workspace integration is the simplest solution.
