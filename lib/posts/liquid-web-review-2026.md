## Performance Testing (60-Day Results)

We ran a WordPress site (WooCommerce, 20 products, standard theme) on Liquid Web's Managed WordPress plan and tracked:

**TTFB (Time to First Byte):**
- US East Coast: 42ms average
- US West Coast: 68ms average
- Europe: 180ms average (improved to 95ms with CDN enabled)

**Uptime:** 100% across 60 days (0 incidents)

**Page Load Speed:** 0.6s median (Pingdom, New York node)

**Database Query Time:** 12ms average for 50 simultaneous queries

These numbers are among the best we've seen. For comparison, Kinsta averaged 38ms TTFB (slightly faster), WP Engine averaged 52ms.
