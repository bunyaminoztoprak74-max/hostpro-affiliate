## Privacy and Security Features

Proton publishes open-source apps for major desktop, mobile and TV platforms. Its apps include WireGuard support, a kill switch and always-on protection. NetShield on paid plans blocks known advertising, tracking and malware domains, but it is not a substitute for endpoint security or safe browsing.

No VPN can prevent phishing, malicious downloads or account takeover after authentication. Use MFA, keep devices patched and follow employer access rules.

## Server Network and Devices

Proton currently advertises more than 20,000 servers across over 140 countries for its paid network, with support for up to 10 simultaneous devices. Those figures change as capacity expands, so confirm the current country list before purchasing for a specific location.

The free plan deliberately limits selection and supports one device. That is reasonable for testing the apps, but frequent travelers and multi-device users will quickly encounter its boundaries.
