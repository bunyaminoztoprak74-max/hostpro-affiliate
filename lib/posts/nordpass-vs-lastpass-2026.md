## Why This Comparison Matters

LastPass was the default recommendation for a decade — until the 2022 breach chain, when attackers stole customer vault backups. Trust never fully recovered. NordPass, built by Nord Security (the NordVPN company), has been the biggest beneficiary. But is it actually better, or just less tainted?

## Security Architecture

| Aspect | NordPass | LastPass |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Encryption | XChaCha20-Poly1305 | AES-256 |
| Zero-knowledge | Yes | Yes |
| Known breaches | None | 2015, 2022 (vault theft) |
| Public audits | Cure53 (2022, 2023) | Yes, post-incident |
| HQ / jurisdiction | Panama | United States |

Both encrypt locally before sync. The difference is track record: NordPass has a clean one.

## Pricing 2026

| Plan | NordPass | LastPass |
|------|----------|----------|
| Free | 1 active device, unlimited passwords | 1 device type only |
| Premium | ~1.99 USD/mo | 3.00 USD/mo |
| Family | ~3.69 USD/mo (6 users) | 4.00 USD/mo (6 users) |

NordPass undercuts LastPass at every tier, and its free plan is less restrictive about device types.

## Daily Usability

Autofill worked reliably in both across Chrome, Firefox, and Edge in our testing. NordPass has the cleaner interface and a genuinely useful extra: **email masking** (disposable addresses that forward to your inbox). LastPass counters with more granular sharing controls for teams.

## Switching Guide (10 Minutes)

1. In LastPass: Account Settings > Advanced > Export > CSV
2. In NordPass: Settings > Import Items > LastPass
3. Verify item count matches, then delete the CSV securely
4. Install the NordPass extension and disable LastPass autofill

## Verdict: NordPass 4.5/5, LastPass 3.6/5

LastPass still works, but you would be paying more for a product with a worse security history. NordPass is cheaper, faster, breach-free, and easier to live with. This is one of the easier switches to recommend in 2026.
