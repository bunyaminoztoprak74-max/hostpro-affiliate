## What Remote Work Actually Demands From a VPN

Forget streaming reviews — remote work stresses a VPN differently: stable latency for video calls, reliable reconnects when switching networks, kill switches so a dropped tunnel never exposes traffic, and speed that does not make cloud drives crawl. We tested with real workloads: Zoom calls, 2 GB file uploads, and a full workday on cafe Wi-Fi.

## 3. Company VPN + Personal VPN Combo

If your employer provides a corporate VPN, run it inside your personal VPN when on public Wi-Fi: connect the personal VPN first (encrypts the network layer), then the corporate client. Both NordVPN and Surfshark handle this stacking without conflicts in our testing.
