## 3–4. Proton VPN and Mullvad

Proton VPN suits buyers who value an integrated privacy ecosystem and a usable free entry point. Mullvad favors a minimal account model and simple pricing philosophy. Neither removes the need to follow corporate security rules.

## 5–6. Tailscale and Cloudflare Zero Trust

These products solve private-resource access rather than merely changing a public IP address. Teams can enforce identity-aware access to internal services without exposing them directly to the internet. Deployment should be owned by the organization's IT or security administrator.

## Remote-Work Security Checklist

- Use the employer-provided connection method.
- Enable phishing-resistant MFA where available.
- Keep full-disk encryption and automatic updates enabled.
- Separate work and personal browser profiles.
- Avoid sensitive calls or screens in public spaces.
- Verify the VPN kill switch before travel.
- Keep recovery codes offline and secure.
