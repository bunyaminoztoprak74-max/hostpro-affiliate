## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I run multiple WordPress sites on cheap hosting?**
Yes. Hostinger's Premium plan ($3.99/month) supports 100 WordPress sites. SiteGround's StartUp only allows 1 site — you'd need the GrowBig plan ($7.99/month) for multiple sites.

**Does cheap WordPress hosting affect my SEO rankings?**
Yes, through page speed. Google's Core Web Vitals (confirmed ranking factors) require TTFB under 200ms and page load under 2.5s for LCP. Hostinger and SiteGround meet these requirements. Bluehost and HostGator often don't without additional plugins.

**What's the best way to speed up WordPress on cheap hosting?**
1. Choose a host with LiteSpeed Cache support (Hostinger)
2. Use a lightweight WordPress theme (Astra, GeneratePress)
3. Optimize images before uploading (or use an image optimization plugin)
4. Limit to essential plugins only (each plugin adds PHP processing time)
5. Use a CDN (free with Hostinger and SiteGround)

**Is free WordPress hosting ever acceptable?**
Only for learning or experimentation. Free WordPress.com (subdomain) or free cPanel hosting from providers like 000webhost is unreliable, slow, shows ads, and lacks the control needed for a real website. At $2.59-2.99/month, paid hosting is affordable enough that free hosting isn't worth the tradeoffs.

**How long should I use cheap hosting before upgrading?**
Consider upgrading when: (1) Your site generates revenue that exceeds the cost of managed hosting, (2) You experience performance issues that caching can't solve, (3) Downtime is affecting your business. For most personal and small business WordPress sites, budget hosting is sufficient indefinitely.
