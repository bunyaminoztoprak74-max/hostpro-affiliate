## Scoring Methodology

Each criterion is graded A+ through D/F:

| Grade | Score | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|

| A | 90-94 | Excellent, above average |
| A- | 85-89 | Very good, minor issues |
| B+ | 80-84 | Good, some weaknesses |
| B | 75-79 | Average for category |
| B- | 70-74 | Below average |
| C | 60-69 | Significant weakness |
| D | 50-59 | Major problem |
| F | < 50 | Unacceptable |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What GPA is acceptable for a web hosting provider?**
We consider B or above acceptable for most use cases. C grades indicate specific weaknesses worth evaluating. Any host with a D grade overall (GoDaddy) or in critical categories (speed, reliability) should be avoided for serious websites.

**Why does Kinsta earn A+ in speed but A in value?**
Kinsta's raw speed is exceptional (A+) but their pricing is premium — at $35/month entry, they're not the best value for low-traffic sites. For high-traffic performance-critical WordPress sites, the value improves as the speed advantage translates to revenue.

**Is Hostinger's B+ in backups a problem?**
Hostinger's base plans include weekly backups — adequate for personal sites, not ideal for business sites. The Business plan upgrades to daily backups. If daily backups are important (they should be for business sites), either upgrade to Hostinger Business or choose SiteGround which includes daily backups on all plans.

**What does a C+ in pricing transparency mean for Bluehost?**
Bluehost aggressively pre-selects paid add-ons (SiteLock, CodeGuard, domain privacy) at checkout and has a significant intro/renewal price gap (272%). These practices earn a C+ — not unethical, but aggressive enough to create surprises for inattentive buyers.

**Should I avoid any host with a C grade in any category?**
Not necessarily — it depends which category. A C in speed might be acceptable if you optimize extensively with plugins and CDN. A C in uptime is more concerning for business sites. A C in support is problematic if you're not technically confident. Evaluate based on your specific priorities.
