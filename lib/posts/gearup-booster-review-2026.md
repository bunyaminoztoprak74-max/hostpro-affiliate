## What Is GearUP Booster?

GearUP Booster is a game-optimized network accelerator (often called a "gaming VPN" though it's more precisely a traffic routing optimizer). It intercepts your game's UDP/TCP traffic and routes it through GearUP's global server network to find the lowest-latency path to the game server — bypassing suboptimal ISP routing that can add unnecessary hops and latency.

It's designed for players who experience high ping, packet loss, or jitter — especially those playing on foreign servers (playing NA servers from Europe or Asia, for example) or those with ISPs known for poor peering agreements.
