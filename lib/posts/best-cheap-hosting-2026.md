## What to Watch Out For with Cheap Hosting

### Hidden renewal prices
Most cheap hosts advertise a promo rate that increases significantly at renewal. Always check the renewal price before signing up. Hostinger and Cloudways have the most honest pricing.

### Storage limits
Some $1-2/month hosts limit you to 5-10GB of storage. For a blog with images this fills up fast. Look for hosts that offer at least 50GB.

### Shared IP and overselling
Very cheap hosts often oversell their servers, meaning your site shares resources with hundreds of other sites. This causes slowdowns during traffic spikes. Hostinger mitigates this with LiteSpeed Cache.

### "Unlimited" claims
Unlimited bandwidth and storage usually have fair-use policies buried in the terms. Read carefully — or choose a host that gives you real numbers.
