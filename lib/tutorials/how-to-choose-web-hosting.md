Choosing web hosting doesn't need to be complicated. Most beginners spend hours comparing dozens of hosts and end up paralyzed by choice. This guide gives you a clear framework.

## The 3 Questions That Determine Your Host

**1. How much traffic do you expect?**
- 0–5,000 visitors/month → Any shared host works
- 5,000–50,000 visitors/month → Good shared hosting (Hostinger) or entry managed cloud (Cloudways)
- 50,000+ visitors/month → Managed cloud or premium managed WordPress

**2. What's your budget?**
- Under $5/mo → Hostinger or Namecheap
- $5–15/mo → Cloudways on DigitalOcean
- $15–50/mo → WP Engine or Kinsta (WordPress only)

**3. How technical are you?**
- No technical skills → Shared hosting with cPanel or hPanel
- Some experience → Cloudways (managed cloud, no server management)
- Developer → VPS or Cloudways

## Hosting Types Explained

### Shared Hosting ($2–5/mo)
Your site shares server resources with hundreds of other sites. Affordable, beginner-friendly, and sufficient for most small sites. Best for: blogs, portfolios, small business sites.

**Best shared host:** [Hostinger](/review/hostinger) — fastest and most affordable in our tests.

### Managed WordPress Hosting ($20–200/mo)
Optimized specifically for WordPress. Includes automatic updates, staging environments, daily backups, and WordPress expert support. Best for: business WordPress sites, WooCommerce stores.

**Best managed WP host:** [WP Engine](/review/wpengine) or [Kinsta](/review/kinsta).

### Managed Cloud Hosting ($11–50/mo)
Your site runs on enterprise cloud infrastructure (AWS, DigitalOcean, Google Cloud), fully managed by the host. Best for: growing sites, agencies, PHP apps.

**Best managed cloud:** [Cloudways](/review/cloudways).

## Specs That Actually Matter

| Spec | What to Look For | What to Ignore |
|------|-----------------|----------------|
| Uptime | 99.9%+ guaranteed | "Unmetered" claims |
| Storage | NVMe SSD > SSD > HDD | Raw GB numbers |
| Bandwidth | Unmetered or 1TB+ | "Unlimited" fine print |
| Support | Under 5 min response | Social media presence |
