### Quora Yanıt #3 — "Cloudways vs WP Engine — which should I choose?"

```
These two are actually built for different users, so the "best" 
one depends entirely on what you're running.

**Choose Cloudways if:**
- You want cloud performance but don't want to manage servers
- You run non-WordPress apps (PHP, Node, Laravel, etc.)
- You want flexibility to choose your cloud provider (DO, AWS, GCP, Vultr)
- Your budget is $11-30/month

**Choose WP Engine if:**
- WordPress is your entire stack
- You're running WooCommerce with significant transactions
- You want the absolute best WordPress-specific tooling 
  (staging, dev/prod workflow, Genesis framework)
- Budget is $20-200/month

**Performance comparison from my tests:**
- Both deliver excellent uptime (99.99%+)
- WP Engine is marginally faster for WordPress specifically
- Cloudways is better for multi-application or custom setups

**Price comparison:**
- Cloudways: starts at $11/mo (pay-as-you-go)
- WP Engine: starts at $20/mo (fixed tiers)

I ran both for 90+ days and wrote a detailed comparison:
https://www.hostproreviews.com/blog/cloudways-vs-wpengine-2026

Happy to answer specific questions about either!
```
