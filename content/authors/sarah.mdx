Sarah has been building, optimizing, and managing WordPress sites professionally for over 8 years. She started her career as a freelance WordPress developer, building sites for small businesses across the UK, before transitioning to content strategy and technical writing focused on web hosting and WordPress performance.

Sarah specializes in managed WordPress hosting, WooCommerce performance optimization, and security hardening for production sites. She has migrated dozens of client sites between hosting providers and maintains her own test environment with active WordPress installations across five different hosts for ongoing benchmark comparisons.

At HostPro Reviews, Sarah reviews all technical content for accuracy, tests tutorial steps on live installations, and writes our in-depth WordPress guides. She ensures every step in our tutorials works on real hosting environments before publication.

## Specialties

**WordPress Performance:** Sarah has a particular focus on Core Web Vitals optimization — she has achieved perfect 100/100 Google PageSpeed scores on multiple WordPress sites using systematic caching, image optimization, and database tuning.

**WooCommerce:** Sarah has built and optimized WooCommerce stores ranging from small boutiques to stores processing 5,000+ orders per month. She understands both the technical and conversion-rate sides of eCommerce hosting.

**Security:** Certified in WordPress security best practices, Sarah reviews hosting providers' security configurations including server-level firewalls, malware scanning, and SSL implementation quality.
